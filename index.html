About

We created HealthCheck with one goal in mind: Make data more accessible. In todays digital world, we take everything for granted, and an example of that is our health information being easily accessible by hospitals. It is critical that doctors can look at past data to aid in diagnosing with possible diseases or chronic illnesses. Not all hospitals can leverage this though. according to https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC5654179/ in sub-Saharan Africa, they "lag in electronic health record adoption" and we want to help with that. HealthCheck provides with secure log-in where we encrypted passwords so in the unlikely event of a data-breach, the hackers will be unable to figure out the credentials. We created our own API so in the future we are able to make changes and keep the code up to date.
